Introduction

Thank you for choosing Simple – your complete design and development toolkit.

Simple is a modern, versatile admin & dashboard template designed to streamline your web application development. Whether you're building analytics tools, SaaS platforms, internal dashboards, or CRM systems, Simple is built to adapt and scale with your project needs.

Crafted with attention to performance, accessibility, and design clarity, Simple is built on the latest technologies, including Bootstrap v5.3.7 and React.js v19. This powerful combination ensures blazing-fast load times, modular architecture, and seamless responsiveness across all devices.

Simple offers a robust UI system with support for multiple themes, skins, layouts, topbars, sidebars, dark mode, RTL, and more. It’s developer-friendly, easy to customize, and comes bundled with production-ready components to help you move faster.

Whether you're a solo developer or working in a team, Simple empowers you to deliver high-quality applications with minimal effort and maximum impact.

We sincerely appreciate your trust in Simple. If you have feedback, questions, or feature requests, we’d love to hear from you. Wishing you success in all your development efforts!
